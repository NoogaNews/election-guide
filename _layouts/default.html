About this guide

There are more than 60 candidates in Hamilton County's Nov. 4 election. Over the past few weeks, Nooga.com sent the candidates brief surveys to help voters get a better understanding of the people who want to represent them in federal, state and local governments. Our guide also features easy-to-read breakdowns of the amendments and ballot measures you'll see on election day.
